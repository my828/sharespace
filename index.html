Introducing hearU

Supporting women leaving abusive relationships

What we do

hearU is a women sponsoring women platform where socially and economically stable women can sponsor, support, and enable women in difficult situations.

Through community support, we help them take the first steps towards leaving while developing their social skills, planning skills, and stability to build long term confidence.

We take out the bureaucracy, wait time, and lack of usability out of resource planning by providing an easy-to-use platform.

Resources We Provide

Micro-Loans

Sometimes women don’t even have the funds to buy a ticket out of town. Not having liquid assets is the major first barrier to getting out. Just a $50 donation can make all the difference.

Community

Abusive relationships can isolate you and leave you without a support system. Building community allows you to develop new skills and provide you the encouragement during a difficult time.

Housing

In situations like these, sometimes it's not rational, safe, or easy to ask a friend or family member to house you. Once you have the space to think, then you can truly start planning for yourself.
